About DIVIT FOUNDATION

Who We Are

A not-for-profit organization making a difference since 2021

DIVIT FOUNDATION is a registered not-for-profit organization committed to creating sustainable social impact across multiple domains. With over 3 years of dedicated service, we have been working tirelessly to empower communities through education, environmental conservation, healthcare initiatives, and academic research.

Our Mission

To create sustainable development by empowering individuals and communities through education, health awareness, environmental conservation, and research initiatives.

Our Vision

A society where every individual has access to quality education, healthcare, and opportunities for growth, living in harmony with nature.

Our Goals

To expand our reach across underserved communities, enhance skill development programs, promote academic research, and create sustainable environmental solutions.
