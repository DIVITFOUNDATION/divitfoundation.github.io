About Us

Divit Foundation is a not-for-profit organization dedicated to empowering communities through education, health, skill development, and sports initiatives. We believe in creating sustainable change by focusing on long-term solutions.

Founded in 2010, we have impacted over 50,000 lives across Assam and beyond. Our mission is to provide access to quality education, healthcare, and opportunities for personal growth.
