About Us

Founded in 2010, Divit Foundation is a not-for-profit organization committed to creating positive social impact through sustainable development initiatives. Our mission is to empower underserved communities by providing access to quality education, healthcare, environmental conservation, and economic opportunities that foster self-reliance and dignity.
