About Us

Our Mission

Divit Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to fostering sustainable development and community empowerment in Northeast India. Established in 2021, we have been working tirelessly to create opportunities for the underprivileged and drive positive change in our communities.

Our approach combines grassroots initiatives with innovative solutions to address the most pressing challenges facing our region. We believe in the power of education, skill development, and environmental stewardship to transform lives and build resilient communities.

Over the years, we have impacted thousands of lives through our diverse programs and initiatives. Our team consists of passionate professionals, educators, and community leaders who bring diverse expertise to our mission.

Our Services

Skill Development Mission

We provide comprehensive training and placement services to empower youth with market-relevant skills. Our programs include technical training, soft skills development, and job placement assistance.

Through partnerships with local industries, we ensure our trainees are job-ready and have access to meaningful employment opportunities.

Academic Research & Publications

We organize seminars and conferences focused on academic research in various fields. Our events bring together scholars, researchers, and industry experts to share knowledge and foster collaboration.

We also publish an in-house journal that showcases cutting-edge research from the Northeast region.

Waste Management Awareness

We conduct awareness seminars and conferences on sustainable waste management practices, with special focus on electronic waste (e-waste). Our initiatives educate communities about responsible disposal and recycling.

We also organize community clean-up drives and establish collection centers for hazardous waste.

Sports & Adventure Activities

Under our brand Borjhar Sports Club, we organize various sporting activities, especially cycling, running, and adventure sports. Our programs promote fitness, teamwork, and community bonding.

We host regular events, marathons, and adventure camps for all age groups.

Our Projects

Vocational Training Centers

We've established vocational training centers across Assam that offer courses in computer skills, tailoring, handicrafts, and hospitality.

E-Waste Management Initiative

Our comprehensive program for responsible e-waste disposal includes collection centers, awareness campaigns, and partnerships with recycling facilities.

Borjhar Sports Events

We organize annual marathons, cycling events, and adventure sports competitions that promote fitness and community participation.

Research

Academic Research Journal

Our in-house research journal publishes peer-reviewed articles on sustainable development, environmental science, social issues, and community development in Northeast India. We provide a platform for researchers to share their findings and contribute to academic discourse.

The journal is published quarterly and is available both in print and digital formats.

Research Conferences

We host annual research conferences that bring together scholars, practitioners, and policymakers to discuss pressing issues facing our region. These conferences feature keynote speakers, paper presentations, and panel discussions on topics ranging from environmental conservation to socioeconomic development.

Our next conference is scheduled for November 2023 with the theme "Sustainable Futures for Northeast India."

Community-Based Research

We conduct field research in collaboration with local communities to understand their needs and develop context-specific solutions. Our participatory research approach ensures that community voices are central to our work.

Current research projects include studies on traditional waste management practices and the impact of vocational training on economic empowerment.
