About Us

Our Vision & Mission

Divit Foundation was established in 2010 with a vision to create a society where every individual has access to opportunities for growth and development.

Our mission is to empower underprivileged communities through education, skill development, environmental conservation, and promoting sports and cultural activities.

Over the years, we have impacted the lives of over 10,000 individuals through our various initiatives and programs across Assam and Northeast India.
