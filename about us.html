About Us

Founded in 2010, Divit Foundation is a not-for-profit organization committed to creating positive social impact through sustainable development initiatives.

Our mission is to empower underserved communities by providing access to quality education, healthcare, environmental conservation, and economic opportunities that foster self-reliance and dignity.

With operations across 15 states and partnerships with over 100 local organizations, we've impacted more than 500,000 lives through our comprehensive programs.
